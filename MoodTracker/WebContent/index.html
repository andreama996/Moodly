Get Help

The National Alliance on Mental Illness works to improve the lives of those living with mental illness by working to change the stigma against mental illness and build up family relationships.

The National Institute of Mental Health is a research organization that works to understand the treatment and prevention of mental disorders. It provides outreach and education opportunities to help people with mental illness feel supported.

The American Psychological Association is a group of clinical psychologists who work with people with mental illness to alleviate symptoms and offer therapy and support.

The Anxiety and Depression Association of America provides education, resources, and treatment for those living with anxiety and/or depression.
